Lemon mousse

Description

Light and fluffy Lemon Mousse is easy to make and delicious, made with cream, lemon and condensed milk.
Itâ€™s an elegant stand alone dessert that can be made ahead.

Ingredients

  • 10.1 FL. OZ Table cream;
  • 14 FL. OZ sweetened condensed milk;
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice;
  • lemon zest.

Steps

  1. Put the cream and condensed milk in the blender;
  2. Blend a little and then add the lemon juice, little by little;
  3. When it gets very consistent, take it to the fridge;
  4. Garnish with lemon zest.
