Brazilian Cheese Bread

Description

Cheese bread, or "Pão de queijo" in Portuguese, is a delightful snack made with tapioca flour.
In Brazil, it is enjoyed all hours of the day, particularly during breakfast and family gatherings.
Pão de queijo é um pequeno pão de queijo assado, um lanche popular e café da manhã no Brasil.
It is a traditional Brazilian recipe, originally from the state of Minas Gerais.

Ingredients

  • 2 20oz sour tapioca starch;
  • 1 cup vegetable oil;
  • 2 cup full fat milk;
  • 3 eggs;
  • 14oz de queijo mussarela;
  • 4 teaspoon salt (or more to taste).

Steps

  1. In a bowl mix the sour tapioca starch and salt.
  2. Boil the milk with the oil.
  3. Pour in the boiled liquid and mix with a large spoon.
  4. Wait to cool down and pour in the grated cheese and eggs.
  5. Mix by hand kneading well.
  6. Make balls the size you prefer.
  7. Bake in a very hot oven until golden brown.
